Research

My research interests lie at the intersection of learning, optimization, control, and simulation for urban mobility systems, with a particular emphasis on Connected and Automated Vehicles (CAVs). Precisely, I aim to advance CAVs' safety through high-fidelity simulation and advanced testing driven by Generative AI, while also pursuing reliable and scalable data-driven control strategies that promote broader societal and environmental benefits.
